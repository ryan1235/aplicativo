# Quickstart Validation Guide

## Validating Custom Notifications

1. **Launch the App**: `python felb_app.py`
2. **Navigate**: Go to the "Notifications" tab.
3. **Create**: Click "Create Custom Notification".
4. **Configure**: Select an image, set duration to 1 minute, toggle "Play Sound" ON, and activate it.
5. **Verify Timer**: Ensure the timer appears in the list and starts counting down.
6. **Verify Alert**: Wait 1 minute. Verify that the visual alert shows and the sound plays.
